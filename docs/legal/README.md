# Legal document drafts — NOT final, NOT legal advice

The three documents in this folder (`PRIVACY_POLICY.md`, `TERMS_OF_SERVICE.md`,
`HIPAA_BAA_TEMPLATE.md`) are **starting-point drafts** generated to describe
what InnovaShift Workforce (ISW) actually does technically — what data it
collects, which Firebase/Stripe subprocessors it uses, how PTO/accrual/payroll
data flows, etc.

They are **not** reviewed by a lawyer and **must not** be published, sent to a
customer, or relied on for compliance purposes until a licensed attorney
(ideally one with healthcare/HIPAA experience, since this product handles
workforce data for healthcare employers) has reviewed and revised them for:

- Your actual business entity name, address, and jurisdiction
- State-specific requirements (e.g. CCPA/CPRA if you have California users,
  other state privacy laws)
- Whether ISW is a "Business Associate" under HIPAA for your specific
  customers (this depends on whether the workforce data you store counts as
  PHI in context — that determination itself needs a lawyer, not this doc)
- Your real subprocessor list, data retention periods, and breach notification
  procedures
- Arbitration/dispute clauses, limitation of liability caps, and indemnification
  language appropriate for your risk tolerance and insurance coverage

Placeholders like `[Company Legal Name]`, `[Jurisdiction]`, `[Contact Email]`,
and `[Effective Date]` are left throughout — fill these in only after legal
review, not before.
